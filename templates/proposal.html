Project Goals

What is the purpose of our project?

Topic

The topic we’ve chosen is about basketball, specifically about how college basketball players translate from college to the NBA. This means we take a look at each player in the NBA today, and compare their career stats in the NBA, and their previous career stats in college. This is the same topic as we’ve chosen for Project 1 for our group, only this time we are now focusing more so on the visualization aspect of the data, as it's the requirement for Project 2. We chose this topic because we were inspired by the variability of something like the NBA Draft, where it is hard for GMs to find out how a player will play in the NBA from college, and we wanted to see if we could help clear up this draft process, and create visualizations, and eventually for Project 3, predictive models that can help tell us how a player will do in the NBA from college stats.

Data

The first dataset is from a sportsreference package that scrapes data fromBasketball-Reference.com, found on PyPi, and includes objects for both NBA players andCollege Players. This gives us most data for stats found in college and in the NBA. This is alsothe same dataset for player stats we used in Project 1. (​https://pypi.org/project/sportsreference/​)
